📋 ABOUT THIS MODEL — Assumptions & Limitations
Physics included: Nernst-Planck transport (full analytical steady-state, Peclet number), Butler-Volmer kinetics, Arrhenius D(T), D(c,T) concentration-dependent diffusivity (Persson 2010 — staging dips), κ(c,T) Valøen-Reimers electrolyte conductivity, SOC-dependent Cp(x) + radiation heat loss (Forgez 2010), mechanistic 3-mechanism cycle fade (SEI+cracking+calendar), mixed parabolic+linear SEI model (Ploehn 2004), Doyle-Newman P2D particle diffusion, Dahn graphite staging OCV
Key assumptions: 1D geometry, dilute solution theory (activity coeff=1), single-particle model per electrode, uniform temperature, parabolic approx for solid diffusion, SEI grows uniformly, no mechanical stress/cracking geometry
Li-ion material: LiCoO₂ cathode (ρ=5.05 g/cm³, Q_th=274 mAh/g) ‖ Graphite anode (D₀=1×10⁻⁹ m²/s, Ea=0.31 eV) | LiPF₆/EC:DMC electrolyte | V window: 3.0–4.2V
Na-ion material: NaMnO₂ cathode (P2-type, ρ=3.8 g/cm³, Q_th=197 mAh/g) ‖ Hard Carbon anode (D₀=3.5×10⁻¹⁰ m²/s, Ea=0.38 eV) | NaClO₄/PC | V window: 2.5–3.8V
Not modeled: Double-layer capacitance, convection, multi-particle distribution, dendrite growth mechanics, full 2D/3D geometry, concentrated solution activity coefficients, mechanical volume expansion
KEY REFERENCES (Li-ion):   Doyle-Fuller-Newman (1993) DFN/P2D · Arrhenius D(T)=D₀exp(−Eₐ/RT) · Persson PRB (2010) D(c) staging dips · Valøen-Reimers J.ES (2005) κ(c,T) · Ploehn J.ES (2004) + Ramadass (2004) SEI · Bernardi Thermal (1985) · Forgez J.PS (2010) Cp(x)+radiation · Ekström-Lindbergh (2015) mechanistic fade · Vetter (2005) · Dahn Graphite Staging (1991) · Orazem EIS (2008) · Ragone (1968) · Waldmann plating (2014)
KEY REFERENCES (Na-ion):   Zhao et al. NaMnO₂ (2012) · Komaba Hard Carbon (2011) · Stevens & Dahn HC (2001) · Slater Na-ion Review (2013) · Delmas P2/O3 phases (1981) · Ponrouch Electrolyte (2013)
